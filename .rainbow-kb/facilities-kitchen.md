# Shared Kitchen & Dining

## Kitchen Facilities (Self-Catering)
- Shared kitchen for guest use
- Refrigerator / freezer
- Microwave
- Electric kettle
- Basic cookware (pots/pans)
- Cooking utensils
- Plates / bowls / cups
- Cutlery

## Water & Drinks
- Hot & cold filtered water dispenser / drinking water station
- Coffee machine (free to use)

## Kitchen Rules
- Clean up after yourself (see `rules-shared-spaces.md`)
- Label your food in the fridge
- No strong-smelling foods (durian, fish)
- Respect "first in, first out" in fridge
