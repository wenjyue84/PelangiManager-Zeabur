## Before Booking

**Q: What is a capsule hotel?**
A: A capsule hotel offers private sleeping pods (capsules) instead of standard rooms. You get your own bed-sized space (with a privacy curtain), while bathrooms and common areas are shared.

**Q: Is it safe and private?**
A: Yes. Each capsule has a privacy curtain, and the property uses key-card access plus CCTV in common areas. Personal lockers are available for valuables.

**Q: How many capsules do you have?**
A: 24 capsule units.

**Q: Can two people share one capsule?**
A: No. One capsule is for one person only.

**Q: Do capsules have doors or curtains?**
A: Curtains (privacy blinds) that you can close fully.

## Location & Getting Here

**Q: What's your address?**
A: Pelangi Capsule Hostel, 26A, Jalan Perang, Taman Pelangi, 80400 Johor Bahru, Johor, Malaysia.

**Q: How do I get there from Senai Airport (JHB)?**
A: Grab/taxi typically takes ~30–40 minutes depending on traffic. There's also an airport shuttle bus to JB Sentral (~45 minutes in good traffic).

**Q: How far is it from JB Sentral / CIQ area?**
A: JB Sentral to Plaza Pelangi is about 4 km and can be as fast as ~5 minutes by bus. From our location, we're about a 15-minute drive to JB Checkpoint (CIQ).

**Q: Is parking available?**
A: Street parking is available around Taman Pelangi (subject to availability).

**Q: What's nearby?**
A: Plenty of eateries and shops in Taman Pelangi, plus the Pelangi night market area.
