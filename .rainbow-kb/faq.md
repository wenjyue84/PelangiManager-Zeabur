## What is a Capsule Hotel?

**Q: What is a capsule hotel?**
A: Private sleeping pods instead of standard rooms. You get your own bed-sized space with a privacy curtain. Bathrooms and common areas are shared.

**Q: How many capsules?**
A: 24 units total.

**Q: Can two people share one capsule?**
A: No. One person per capsule only.

**Q: Doors or curtains?**
A: Privacy curtains that close fully.

## Safety & Privacy

**Q: Is it safe and private?**
A: Yes. Privacy curtain, key-card access, CCTV in common areas, and personal lockers for valuables.

**Q: Safe for female solo travelers?**
A: Yes — same security setup applies to all guests.

## Guest Types

**Q: Good for solo travelers?**
A: Yes — many solo travelers stay here. Common area is great for meeting others.

**Q: Can you host groups?**
A: Yes. Contact us and we'll try to place capsules near each other (subject to availability).

**Q: Can families with children stay?**
A: Yes, but each person (including children) needs their own capsule.

**Q: Minimum age?**
A: 18+ (minors need parent/guardian).
