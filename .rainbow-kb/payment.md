## 2) Deposits & Additional Charges

### Security Deposit (Refundable)

- **RM10 card deposit** (standard stays)
- **RM100 security deposit** (long-term stays)
- Used to cover: damages, missing items, unpaid charges, or excessive cleaning
- Refunded at the end of stay if no deductions are required

### Common Additional Charges (If Applicable)

- Key card replacement: **RM10**
- Late checkout: **Free** (subject to availability)
- Early check-in: **Free** (subject to availability)
- Extra cleaning required: **RM20+** (depends on work needed)
- Damage / missing items: **actual cost** (evidence-based)

> We don't like random charges. If we charge you, we should be able to explain it clearly.

## 4) When Payment Is Due

### Standard Stays

- **Full stay payment** is due **before or at arrival**
- **Card deposit (RM10)** is collected together (unless waived by management)

### Long Stays / Extensions

- Pay **upfront** OR pay **weekly in advance** (every 7 days)
- Any extension must be paid **before** the extension begins
- Long-term stays (1+ month): **RM100 deposit** + first month payment

1. Open your eWallet/banking app → Scan QR
2. Confirm recipient shows **"Pelangi Capsule Hostel"**
3. Enter amount and pay
4. **Send proof of payment** (screenshot) to staff

### Bank Transfer Steps

1. Transfer to **Maybank 551128652007**
2. Use **Reference/Note** format:
    - `NAME + CHECKIN DATE`
    - Example: `ALI 12FEB`
3. Send screenshot/receipt to staff for verification

### Proof of Payment (What We Need)

- Screenshot showing:
    - amount
    - date/time
    - recipient name/account
    - transaction reference

> No proof = we treat it as unpaid. Bank apps don't "promise", they only "confirm".

## 6) Refunds & Returns

### A) Security Deposit Refund (RM10 or RM100)

**Full refund if:**

- no damage / missing items
- no unpaid charges

**Partial refund if:**

- deductions for cleaning/damage apply (we'll explain what and why)

**Refund method:**

- cash OR bank transfer (depending on operational practicality)
- typical processing for bank transfer: **1–3 business days**

### B) Booking / Prepayment Refunds

**STRICTLY NO REFUND** for all bookings and prepayments.

- All payments are **non-refundable** once made
- No exceptions for cancellations, changes, or no-shows
- Please ensure your booking details are correct before payment

> If your booking came via an OTA ([Booking.com](http://Booking.com) / Agoda / etc.), their refund policies apply and are managed by the platform.
> 

---

## 7) Cancellation & Changes (Direct Bookings Only)

### Cancellation

- **No refunds** for cancellations at any time
- Payment is forfeited upon cancellation

### Date Changes

- **>7 days before arrival:** free date change (subject to availability)
- **<7 days:** RM10 admin fee + subject to availability
- Changes must be requested in advance

> Date changes do not guarantee refunds. Unused nights are forfeited.
> 

---

## 8) Disputes & Charge Questions

If you disagree with a charge:

1. Raise it immediately (the sooner, the easier to verify)
2. Request an itemized breakdown
3. Management reviews within **24 hours**
4. Evidence can include photos, timestamps, and payment logs
