## Booking / Prepayment Refunds

**STRICTLY NO REFUND** for all bookings and prepayments.
- All payments are **non-refundable** once made
- No exceptions for cancellations, changes, or no-shows
- Please ensure your booking details are correct before payment

> If your booking came via an OTA (Booking.com / Agoda / etc.), their refund policies apply and are managed by the platform.

---

## Cancellation & Date Changes (Direct Bookings Only)

### Cancellation
- **No refunds** for cancellations at any time
- Payment is forfeited upon cancellation

### Date Changes
- **>7 days before arrival:** Free date change (subject to availability)
- **<7 days:** RM10 admin fee + subject to availability
- Changes must be requested in advance

> Date changes do not guarantee refunds. Unused nights are forfeited.

---

## Disputes & Charge Questions

If you disagree with a charge:
1. Raise it immediately (the sooner, the easier to verify)
2. Request an itemized breakdown
3. Management reviews within **24 hours**
4. Evidence can include photos, timestamps, and payment logs
