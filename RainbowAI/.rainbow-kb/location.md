## üá∏üá¨ From Singapore (Most Common!)

Many of our guests arrive from Singapore! Here are the best ways:

### Option 1: Public Bus (Cheapest - S$2.60-4.80)

**Route via Kranji MRT:**
1. Take MRT to **Kranji Station**
2. Exit and find **Causeway Link CW1** bus stop
3. Board CW1 to Johor Bahru CIQ
4. **Cost:** S$2.60
5. **Duration:** 5-10 mins to Woodlands + immigration time
6. From JB CIQ, take bus/Grab to hostel (see below)

**Route via Queen Street (24-Hour Service!):**
1. Go to **Queen Street Bus Terminal** (Bugis area)
2. Take **Causeway Link CW2** (operates round-the-clock!)
3. **Cost:** S$4.80
4. **Duration:** ~30 mins to Woodlands Checkpoint + immigration
5. Night service: 11pm-4:15am (45-min intervals)

**After clearing JB Immigration:**
- Take Grab from JB CIQ (~RM10-15, 10 mins)
- OR take bus CT1/J11/J15/J20/J22 toward Taman Pelangi (see "From JB Sentral" below)

### Option 2: KTM Shuttle Tebrau Train (Fastest Transit - ~S$5)
1. Take MRT to **Woodlands Checkpoint**
2. Clear Singapore customs
3. Take **KTM Shuttle Tebrau** train to **JB Sentral**
4. **Duration:** 5 minutes train ride (plus immigration queues)
5. From JB Sentral, see section below

### Option 3: Private Car/Taxi (Most Comfortable - S$60-100)
1. Book cross-border taxi from Singapore
2. Direct door-to-door service
3. **Cost:** S$60-100 (varies by pickup location)
4. Best for groups or lots of luggage!
