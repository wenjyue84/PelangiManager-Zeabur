## When Payment Is Due

**Standard Stays:**
- Full stay payment due **before or at arrival**
- Card deposit (RM10) collected together (unless waived)

**Long Stays / Extensions:**
- Pay **upfront** OR **weekly in advance** (every 7 days)
- Extensions must be paid **before** extension begins
- Long-term (1+ month): RM100 deposit + first month payment
